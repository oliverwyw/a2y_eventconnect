42nd Street
7714 Ann Arbor St., Dexter, MI 48130
Various Times June 1, 2023 - June 25, 2023
Come and meet those dancing feet on 42ND STREET, the glamorous, Tony Award-Winning, 1930’s musical spectacular. The stakes are high when a young chorus girl fresh from Allentown, PA, is given the once-in-a-lifetime chance to become a star.         This show runs from June 1 – 25, 2023, on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays.
