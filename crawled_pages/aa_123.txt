Ypsilanti Community Choir Concert Series
Towsley Auditorium, 4800 E Huron River Dr, Ann Arbor , MI 48105
7:30 PM 5/11/2023, 12/7/2023
Attend a free concert organized by the Ypsilanti Community Choir!  Concerts through 2023 will take place at Towsley Auditorium on the campus of Washtenaw Community College  Winter 2022– Thursday, December 15 Spring 2023- Thursday, May 11 Winter 2023-  Thursday, December 7  All concerts begin at 7:30 pm. Admission is free.  Please visit the website for more information and to purchase tickets.
