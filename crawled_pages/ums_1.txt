NT Live: Frankenstein on Halloween
Michigan Theater
2022-10-31T19:30
Childlike in his innocence but grotesque in form, Frankenstein’s bewildered creature is cast out into a hostile universe by his horror-struck maker. Meeting with cruelty wherever he goes, the increasingly desperate and vengeful Creature is determined to track down his creator and strike a terrifying deal. Benedict Cumberbatch plays The Creature while Jonny Lee Miller plays the role of Victor Frankenstein.
