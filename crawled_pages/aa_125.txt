Ypsilanti Jazz Festival
2 E Cross St , Ypsilanti, MI 48197
6:00 PM to 9:00 PM 7/1/2023, 7/8/2023, 7/15/2023, 7/29/2023, 8/5/2023, 8/12/2023, 8/19/2023
Save the date! The Ypsilanti Jazz Festival returns for its second year at Riverside Park! Music begins at 7 PM. Bring your own chairs and enjoy free summer music from some of the best jazz artists in the area.   Saturday Dates: July 1, July 8, July 15, July 29, August 5, August 12, and August 19 (There will not be a show on July 22)  Special Bands TBD.
