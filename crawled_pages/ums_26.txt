You Can Dance–Outside! Ypsilanti: Hip Hop and Afrobeat
Riverside Park Pavilion
2022-09-03T10:00
This year, UMS is doubling down on the dance fun by offering two dance sessions! Each day will begin with a 45-minute Introduction to Dance workshop that creates space for participants ages six and up to ground themselves in the basics of the movement form. This will be followed by an hour-long Curious Movers workshop for participants ages 12 and up who want to deepen their dance experience through further exploration of forms and rhythms.10–10:45 am // Introduction to Hip Hop &amp; Mindfulness // Ages 6+11–12 noon // Curious Movers: Afrobeat // Ages 12+
