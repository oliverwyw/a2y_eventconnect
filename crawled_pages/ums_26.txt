You Can Dance: Detroit Jit 22/23
Riverside Arts Center
2023-02-11T11:00
Facilitated by artists from across the Midwest, these 60-minute workshops are ideal for movers seeking to deepen their awareness of movement and music through fun and engaging experiences. Recommended for participants ages 12 and up; little to no dance experience is required.In this class, participants will learn about a Detroit dance form known as Jit. We&#8217;ll begin with a follow-the-leader warm-up utilizing foundational movements, learn a few small combinations, and piece together a short routine.
