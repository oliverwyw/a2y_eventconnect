UMS Presents: Power Moves: Breakdancing & Graffiti Art
100 Market Pl, Ypsilanti, MI 48198
11:00 AM April 23, 2023
U-M University Musical Society and the City of Ypsilanti announce a new partnership bringing performances and events for all ages to the Ypsilanti Freighthouse! Expect arts programming beginning with this week-long pilot program, April 22-29, 2023.  Local movement artist Maurice Archer will perform a breakdancing demonstration and lead families in an interactive workshop. Immediately after, families will participate in an art-making workshop with a graffiti artist who will guide them through developing breakdancing names and creating their own unique signature (or tag).  Please visit the website to join the interest list. Priority registration for Ypsilanti residents opens March 14.
