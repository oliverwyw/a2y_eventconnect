Historic Preservation Night: The San Joaquin Siren
47884 D St, Belleville, MI 48111
7:30 PM to 9:00 PM August 2, 2023
Join in at the Yankee Air Museum for the story of the San Joaquin Siren, presented by Dave Steiner as he talks about his uncle, Captain Bill Behrns, who completed 104 combat missions flying P-38's in the China Burma India Theater with the 459th Fighter Squadron, The Twin Dragons.  Please visit the website for more information and to register.
