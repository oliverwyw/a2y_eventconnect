An Evening with Natalie Merchant - Keep Your Courage Tour
603 E Liberty Street, Ann Arbor, MI 48104
8:00 PM May 17, 2023
Singer-songwriter Natalie Merchant is returning to the road with her first full album of new songs in nearly a decade. Natalie is best known as the lead singer with the alternative rock band,10,000 Maniacs. Natalie Merchant’s Keep Your Courage is her ninth solo studio album and first of new material since 2014’s self-titled record.
