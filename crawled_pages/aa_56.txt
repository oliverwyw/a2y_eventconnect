UMS Presents: Open Mic Night
100 Market Pl, Ypsilanti, MI 48198
7:30 PM April 28, 2023
U-M University Musical Society and the City of Ypsilanti announce a new partnership bringing performances and events for all ages to the Ypsilanti Freighthouse! Expect arts programming beginning with this week-long pilot program, April 22-29, 2023. Bring yourself, your friends, and whatever unique creative contribution you can offer!   This local artist showcase and open mic will be hosted by Ypsilanti resident and singer/songwriter Rochelle Clark. Those who wish to perform — music, dance, comedy, poetry, juggling, or whatever unique creative contribution you can offer — will sign up for a ten-minute slot upon arrival; names will be randomly selected for a starring moment on stage at the Ypsilanti Freighthouse.  Stay late for a 10 PM late night DJ set with Todd Osborn!
