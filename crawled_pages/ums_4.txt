Are we not drawn onward to new erA
Power Center
2023-01-20T20:00
The Belgian theater collective Ontroerend Goed traffics in the unpredictable. Like its title, Are we not drawn onward to new erA unfolds as a palindrome. This striking theatrical masterwork comes to Ann Arbor following its US premiere at the Brooklyn Academy of Music, offering an impossible chance to turn back the clock.
