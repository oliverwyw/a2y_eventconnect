Power Moves: Breakdancing, Art, and Creativity
Ypsilanti Freighthouse
2023-04-23T11:00
Local movement artist Maurice Archer will perform a breakdancing demonstration and lead families in an interactive workshop. Immediately after, families will participate in an art-making workshop with Curtis Wallace, who will guide participants through developing breakdancing names and creating their own unique signature (or tag).
