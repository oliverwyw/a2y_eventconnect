Choral Concert: Come Rain or Come Shine
1500 Scio Church Rd, Ann Arbor, MI 48103
3:00 PM to 4:15 PM April 30, 2023
Washtenaw Chorale Spring Concert, in honor of its long-awaited return, entitled “Come Rain or Come Shine!” It will include music by Daniel Elder, Oja Gleilo, Haydn, Robert Lowery, Johnny Mercer, Thomas Morley, and others.
