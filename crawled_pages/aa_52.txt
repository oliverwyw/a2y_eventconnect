Ann Arbor Summer Festival
881 N University Ave., Ann Arbor, MI 48109
 June 9, 2023 - June 25, 2023
A Community Tradition since 1984 Ann Arbor Summer Festival champions performing arts, outdoor entertainment, and community spirit.  A nearly four-week festival each June that attracts a diverse audience of over 80,000 people and offers over 200 concerts, art exhibitions, kids activities, spectacle, and film screenings. THU JUNE 15An Evening With Renée Elise Goldsberry FRI JUNE 16Reggie Watts SUN JUNE 25Jason Isbell and the 400 Unit with Peter One
