Online Art Classes: Exploring Warm and Cool Colors in the Medium of Your Choice
Ann Arbor, MI 48104
7:30 AM to 8:30 AM April 13, 2023 - May 4, 2023
Exploring Warm and Cool Colors in the Medium of Your ChoiceApril 11th-May 2nd with Sara Wilson (Tuesday Nights from 7:30PM-8:30PM EST)Sara has many years of experience working with patients at the Rudolf Steiner Health Center as our art therapist. Her gentle guidance and deep insights will create an atmosphere of confidence for beginners and seasoned artists alike. This class will work with warm and cool colors in a medium of your choice (she will help you pick if you don't have a preference). Tips will go out prior to the first class. Be sure to sign up as early as possible to reserve your spot, This program is priced on a sliding scale from $45-$85 to make it accessible to as many people as possible.
