Cold Storage Tour Of Cold War Warriors
801 Kirk Profit Drive, Ypsilanti, MI 48197
Various Times 6/24/2023, 9/23/2023
The Yankee Air Museum is bringing you the rare opportunity to get up close and personal with some of the amazing Cold War Warriors. Explore jets that transformed the military and tour an aircraft that flew missions 24/7. Get close to the iconic F-101 Voodoo, the F-86 Sabre and even the EC-121K Warning Star that are typically closed to the public. Visit the website for more information.
