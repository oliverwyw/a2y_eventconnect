ANN ARBOR Area EVENTS

No matter the season, Ann Arbor celebrates life and culture with a wide array of world-class events. Art, theater, athletics, music, academics, history, and more—indulge all of your passions through our eclectic mix of small-town charm and energizing city life. Plan your visit any time of year and discover the special kind of magic that only a place like Ann Arbor can create.
