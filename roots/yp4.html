From the various pop-up sales to the funkiest indie art fairs you can imagine, Ypsilanti’s events are 100% unique. Join us for classic car shows, craft beer festivals, air shows, Mad Hatter tea parties, puppeteering theatrical performances and more, year-round in historic and proud Ypsilanti.
